Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 September 2024

1. Introduction

At Taubman Approach AI Expert, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and protection of your data when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We collect and store IP addresses to manage daily usage limits.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information on how you interact with our website and AI assistant.
  • Cookies: We do not currently use cookies, but we may implement them in the future to enhance user experience.

3. How We Use Information

  • Daily Usage Limitation: We use IP addresses to enforce our daily request limit of 10 queries per user.
  • Service Improvement: We may analyze usage patterns to improve our AI assistant and website functionality.
  • Communication: If you contact us, we may use your information to respond to your inquiries.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Third-party Service Providers: We use OpenRouter to process AI queries. They may have access to the content of your messages but not to your personal information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Retention

IP addresses are reset every 24 hours. We do not retain historical data about individual user queries beyond this period.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it.
  • Opt-out of any future communications from us.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure data transmission using HTTPS.
  • Limited access to personal information by our staff.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where our AI processing service is located. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us to have it removed.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of our service after such modifications will constitute your acknowledgment of the modified Privacy Policy.
